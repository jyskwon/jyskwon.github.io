About

I am an Assistant Professor of Information and Interaction Design (IID) major at Yonsei University, Underwood International College, Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences (HASS) Division. Before joining Yonsei, I was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at DGIST. My research interests are focused on combining Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) methodologies to harness efficient and effective human-computer collaboration. I manage DIAG (Designing Intelligence Augmentation Group) where we study and build hybrid intelligence systems — combining human and machine intelligence to understand and overcome challenging computational problems.

Research Keywords: #Human Computation; #Crowdsourcing; #Human-Computer Interaction; #Artificial Intelligence
